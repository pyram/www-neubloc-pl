Neubloc specializes in setting up outsourced development teams for small and medium sized software product companies. We provide you with a low-risk solution with minimal capital investment, helping you to reserve equity and cash, focus on innovation, leverage our experience and infrastructure and minimize your time and effort.

Build

We get your development operations started quickly by using our existing infrastructure. We hire new team resources based on specific needs to ensure possession of a proper domain experience, as opposed to picking from an existing pool of consultants.

Operate

We provide and modify processes that align with yours to ensure offshore resources are integrated tightly. After process integration and setup, we build the team to the desired size and strength.

Transfer

After a specified time frame that aligns with your company objectives, we help you incorporate your offshore company, set up facilities for you and transfer all the resources.
