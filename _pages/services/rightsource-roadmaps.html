Neubloc's RightSource Roadmap offering provides our clients with a high-level 18 month plan for outsourcing appropriate portions of their product design and engineering operations. In addition to proper documentation and training, this plan includes key activities implemented prior to outsourcing.

Our consulting services:

  • An analysis of your strategic goals to determine an appropriate outsourcing plans.
  • An analysis of existing and future products to determine their outsourcing probability. Some of the key factors include the product's maturity stage; team size, culture & composition; specialty/domain skills; and Intellectual Property constraints.
  • An analysis of the technical and domain knowledge skill sets required to perform potential outsourced work.
  • Analysis of your average personnel cost of necessary roles in order to determine the financial status appropriate for outsourcing those roles.
  • Analysis of the roles and positions that might not currently be addressed.

Our RightSource Roadmap offering provides you with insight to collapse your time-to-market, save on operation costs, and guidance to where you should place your key resources.
