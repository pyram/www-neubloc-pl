Technical Project Manager

The Project Manager will be responsible for client interaction/communication, requirements gathering, and assisting client in the development and tracking of multiple project plans. The Project Manager will work with the client to ensure successful completion of projects as measured by customer satisfaction, achieving measurable goals and on-time delivery.

Responsibilities:

  • Works with client project managers to define, develop & track project plans
  • Responsible for day-to-day oversight of various projects
  • Ensure proper communications between client and offshore teams
  • Ensuring process and methodology guidelines are followed
  • Produce proposals, presentations and status reports
  • Responsible for maintaining and reporting on customer satisfaction
  • Responsible for producing and presenting Weekly Project Dashboard Reports.

Qualifications:

  • 5+ years of software development experience
  • 3 years of embedded software development experience
  • 3 years of project management experience
  • Must have previous experience managing multiple software development projects
  • Great client communications skills
  • Demonstrated leadership skills, customer service attitude and attention to details
  • Writing and presentation skills
  • Past experience working with offshore teams highly desirable
  • BS/BA in computer science or related experience.
Location: Orange County, CA (Lake Forest)

Senior Software Engineer (Contract Position)

Neubloc is looking for a seasoned Ada developer to support a current customer contract. This is an exciting opportunity for the right individual to be a part of a dynamic and fast-paced project.

Requirements:

  • 10+ years Ada programming experience, including expertise with ActivAda and Ada83 related compilers
  • 5-10+ years embedded development experience
  • 5-10+ years real-time operating system experience
  • Experience as a contractor, supporting a customer team
  • Authorized to work in the U.S.
  • Ability to travel to Houston approximately once a month - can be based anywhere in the United States.

If you meet these requirements, we want to hear from you!
