If you are looking for a way to reduce your start-up costs or you have a great idea for the next killer product, our Equity Option Program may be your solution. We now offer a program designed specifically for startups to reduce conflicts and improve productivity.

Program Details

Neubloc is a rightsource software product design and development firm that leverages its US-based executive management, architecture, and design with off shore production and engineering resources to provide successful, cost-conscious solutions for our clients. We use a hybrid mix of resources here in the US and abroad to ensure you receive proper and relevant resources with the utmost efficiency. You will be impressed by our outstanding and extremely competitive rates.

Throughout the process, our diversely talented resources can provide assistance in user experience, design, architecture, and software development efforts in a variety of technologies. These range from Java, .Net, Flash/Flex and mobile applications.

Special Equity Option Program

Neubloc understands the challenges beginning stage startup companies face in bringing their product and vision to market. We have created a special equity option program for qualified recipients to leverage your cash flow, while working hand in hand with angel and VC groups to gain additional funding. Our co-investment model is a mutual beneficial commitment to product development that makes sense for all parties by assessing the overall development needs and the ratio of cash required to complete the project in determining an equity commitment.

Our program is offered in a convertible note for the value of the investment portion we provide, greatly reducing the need for evaluations at a time when determining a firm's value can pose unnecessary complications.
